The Tortoise and Hare

The story of The Tortoise and the Hare" is a classic fable from Aesop. In this tale, a speedy hare mocks a slow-moving tortoise for his lack of speed. The tortoise, tired of the teasing, challenges the hare to a race. Confident in his ability, the hare sprints ahead and, sure that he will win easily, decides to take a nap midway through the race.
